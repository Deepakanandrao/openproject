Ticket: https://community.openproject.org/wp/68031

Summary
Ensure toggleable fieldsets have their event listeners attached exactly once on `turbo:load` to avoid duplicate bindings and broken toggling when navigating with Turbo.

What and why
- Re-attaches the initialization of toggleable fieldsets to the `turbo:load` event so listeners are only bound once.
- Partially reverts changes from #19495 which caused listeners to be attached multiple times or removed incorrectly.
- Addresses the bug where fieldsets did not toggle after Turbo navigations.

Testing & compatibility
- Manual testing with Turbo navigation recommended across major browsers.
- Add/update automated tests if possible.
